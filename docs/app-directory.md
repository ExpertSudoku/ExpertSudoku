## Detailed description

**One grid a day. Pick your pain.**

ExpertSudoku serves one sudoku per day in three difficulties: MediumSudoku
(singles only — a clean warm-up), ExpertSudoku (pairs, pointing pairs,
box/line reduction) and HellSudoku (everything else — bring coffee). Everyone
in the world plays the same three grids; new puzzles arrive at 00:00 UTC.

**Play together, spoil nothing.** Launch the Activity from any channel and a
live board appears in chat: it shows which cells each player has filled —
never the digits — alongside avatars and running times. Up to four boards
race side by side. You can watch a friend close in on a solve without ever
seeing their answers.

**Keep the streak alive.** Every day at least one member solves a puzzle,
your server's streak grows. Miss a day, and it resets. Each night the bot
posts the day's results with a leaderboard of the fastest solvers.

**Built for the way you actually play.** Progress saves automatically — leave
mid-puzzle and pick it up later, on the same board. Pausing hides the grid
(no cheating at the office). Solved a puzzle? Revisit your solution any time,
or jump straight into the next difficulty.

No accounts, no ads, no data sold — ever. Also playable in the browser at
https://expertsudoku.app.
